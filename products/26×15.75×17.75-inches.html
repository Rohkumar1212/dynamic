Box Making – Details & Features

This RSC carton is produced using industrial corrugation technology and is available in double-wall grade. We use high-quality kraft paper facings and water-based adhesive.

  • Design: Regular Slotted Container (RSC)
  • Seams: Hot melt or cold glue
  • Testing: Lightweight ECT range available
  • Finishing: Plain kraft, printed, single-colour branding

Specifications & Key Features

Fluting : BC (double-wall)

Paper Grades: 120–250 GSM kraft liners

Finishing: Plain

Strength: ECT 32–44 / Burst 300–600 kPa

Shipping & Minimum Order

India-wide shipping available. MOQ starts from 50 units for custom-size boxes. Bulk orders are palletized for safe handling.

Perfect for:
